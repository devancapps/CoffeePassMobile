The Reality

Running a great café isn't
the hard part. Getting found is.

Independent cafés make better coffee than the chains. But chains have marketing budgets, apps, and loyalty programs. You have word of mouth and hope.

📣

Discovery is broken for indie cafés

Google and Yelp surface whoever pays for ads — usually the chain next door. New customers can't find you even when they're looking for exactly what you offer.

💸

Marketing is expensive and hard to measure

Instagram ads, Google ads, Yelp — $300-1,500 per month with no guarantee of a single new customer. And you're competing with brands that outspend you 100x.

📱

No digital ordering means lost sales

Customers who can order ahead from their phone will choose the place that lets them. Without mobile ordering, you're invisible to an entire generation of coffee buyers.

FAQ

Common questions
from café owners.

Nothing upfront. Zero monthly fee. Zero setup cost. CoffeePass takes 12% on orders that come through the app. If you receive zero orders, you pay zero. The only cost is a percentage of revenue you wouldn't have had otherwise.
Customers buy credit bundles from CoffeePass. Each credit is worth $1. When a customer orders a $6 drink from your café, they spend 6 credits. You receive $5.28 (88 cents per credit). You never touch credits — you just receive your payout in dollars every Monday via direct deposit.
Yes, completely. You set every item's credit price on your menu. A $5 latte could be 5 credits, 6 credits, or 7 credits — your choice. We recommend pricing close to your cash prices to keep it straightforward for customers, but there are no restrictions.
Every Monday, CoffeePass calculates your previous week's completed orders and deposits your earnings directly into your linked bank account via Stripe. No manual requests. No invoices. Just a deposit every week.
